Paragraph structure

How to use Paragraph structure

Steps

  1. Make a Point: State the first point you're making. This should connect back to the first point in your plan of development.
  2. Proof: Give an example, statistic, or fact that supports your point and proves it's true.
  3. Explain: Explain how your proof supports the point and clearly connect it back to your original idea.

Example of a PPE Paragraph

Point: Harassment is a serious issue that affects many students in schools today.
Proof: According Tulane University , 1 in 4 people experience harassment, and many of them suffer from mental health disorders because of it.
Explanation: This shows that harassment can make students feel unsafe and fearful in their school community. When students don't feel safe, it becomes harder for studnets to learn and enjoy school. That’s why it’s important for schools to take action and prevent harassment before it causes long-term damage.

What are key fetures to use PPE

    • 1. Make a claear point
    • 2. Strong proof
    • 3. Detailed Explanation

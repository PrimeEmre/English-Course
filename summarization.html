Summarization

Steps for Summarization

  • Read the Text Carefully (Go through the text slowly and understand the main ideas. – Focus on what’s important.)
  • Highlight or Note Key Points (Pick out the most important facts, ideas, or events.Ignore tiny details that don’t matter.)
  • Put It in Your Own Words (Rewrite the key points using your own words.– Keep it short and simple.)
  • Check for Accuracy (Make sure your summary is true to the original text. Check if you’ve included all the important ideas.)
  • Shorten and Finalize (Remove repeating ideas and filler words.  – Make sure your summary is clear and easy to understand.)

    Visually summarizing


    Steps for Visually summarizing

    • Read and Watch Carefully (Go through the text or video first to understand the main ideas and key details.)
    • Highlight the Main Points (Pick out the biggest ideas, important facts, or moments that stand out.)
    • Decide on a Visual Format: (Choose how you’ll show it — like a poster, comic, mind map, timeline, or infographic.)
    • Sketch It Out: (Draw a rough layout with spaces for text, images, and connections between ideas.)
    • Use Simple Text and Images (Keep words short and clear, and use symbols or drawings to support your ideas.)
    • Add Colors and Labels: (Make it attractive and easy to understand by using colors, titles, and labels.)
    • Check if It’s Clear: (Make sure someone can understand the main ideas just by looking at your visual.)

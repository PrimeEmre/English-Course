Craft Move Definition Why use it?
Problem & Solution When the writer is presenting the topic as a problem, but then also providing clear solutions to the problem We use problem and solution so that the individual can better understand that the topic is not out of anyone’s hands, that there are things we can do to change the circumstances. Reader might better engage with the topic if they know they are able to contribute to the change.
Chronological When presenting information in order (time, year, steps). he writer might use this when they want to provide clear instructions for something. Providing the topic’s information from beginning to end might provide context for the event/ topic and might give the reader’s a hint to the cause and effect.
Cause & Effect When the topic is presented by showcasing that actions have certain consequences. The writer might use this in order to show that this particular instance has really strong effects. It also allows the reader to infer the relationship between concepts or ideas.
Description Providing a very clear description of the event/ topic. The writer uses this to be very clear about the topic to provide all the relevant and important information the reader needs. The writer might provide examples, describe why the topic is important.
Compare & Contrast Comparing multiple events/ information and giving a clear description as to why they are similar and how they are different. More importantly, compare and contrast is not always just positives and negatives, or similarities and differences, it can just compare two events/ things/ topics in general. The writer’s might use this structure to allow the reader to make their own connections between events, topics or ideas. For example, comparing Nike to Adidas.

How to create a sentence using these craft moves

One evening, a group of friends was sitting in a coffee shop, chatting about their school lives. As one person shared a memory from his school days, another suddenly punched him without any reason. The man fell to the ground, shocked and confused, wondering why he was attacked. A few minutes later, the police arrived and started investigating the scene. They asked witnesses what happened and reviewed the coffee shop’s security footage. It turned out the attacker had misunderstood something during the conversation and reacted violently. Because he physically harmed someone without cause, he was arrested and charged with harassment.
